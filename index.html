Phases of Technical Readiness

Feature Technical Readiness Levels

0 - Research
No technical implications and possibilities
1 - Minimum Viable Functionality
Not applicable to a use realistic case yet.
2 - Minimum Viable Product - Alpha
Integrated with other functions; user data stored, works in ecosystem
3 - Minimum Viable Product - Beta
Works for active use and support of userbase
4 - Minimum Viable Product - Release Candidate
Works with improved models and testing; works for additional use cases deemed relevant
5 - Viable Product - LTS
Works in continual deployment environment with feature patches and upgrades based on predicted long-term support (LTS) requirements in consumer ecosystems.
